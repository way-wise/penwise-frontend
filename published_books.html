Coffee, Heels & Complications

Romance

Chloe has always dreamed of fashion, but working behind the counter of a trendy New York café, her talent seemed destined to remain invisible. Everything changes the day she stumbles upon Alexander’s forgotten sketchbook—a collection of raw, breathtaking concepts that spark both inspiration and an unexpected connection. Determined to chase her ambitions, Chloe enters Sofia Delacroix’s fiercely competitive mentorship program, only to face Victoria Sterling, a ruthless rival willing to sabotage her every step. Between plagiarism accusations, crushing critiques, and the risk of losing her confidence, Chloe must fight harder than ever to claim her place in the industry. Meanwhile, Alexander struggles with family pressures to abandon photography for the safe world of hotel management, even as Chloe reignites his creative soul.

The Curator's Collection

Mystery

A renowned art authenticator awakens trapped in a mansion where each room recreates crime scenes from his career, forcing him to confront his dark past to escape his vengeful captor—the daughter of a murdered gallery owner whose death he helped cover up. When a morally compromised art authenticator is imprisoned by the daughter of a woman he helped murder, he must solve deadly puzzles based on stolen masterpieces within 72 hours, using his expertise to decode clues while confronting the three crimes that built his fortune—or face the same fate as his victims.

3 girls who visit lots of planets

Youth

Emma, Zoé and Lina are three inseparable best friends. One summer evening, they discover a strange silver capsule at the bottom of the garden, glowing in the moonlight. Without really understanding how, they activate a dashboard full of colorful buttons... and there they are, propelled into space! For each planet they visit, a unique adventure awaits them. On one planet covered entirely in ice, they meet penguins who speak in song. On another, made of luminous forests, they must help tiny creatures find their missing queen. Then there's the planet of the giants, where everything is huge - even the sweets, which are crunched like watermelons. Through these journeys, the three friends learn to stick together, use their differences as a strength and understand that friendship is the greatest power of all. But the capsule seems to have a will of its own... and the girls will have to solve its mystery if they ever want to return home.

The wolves of winter

Crime

A former KGB officer, bitter and disgraced, decides to take down an oligarch who betrayed his country to get rich. He recruits three dangerous former comrades to steal the man’s personal gold reserve, hidden inside a heavily guarded warehouse on the outskirts of Moscow. But every man in this crew has his own demons… and the Russian winter shows no mercy. Moscow, winter 1993. Russia is in chaos: communism has collapsed, fortunes are made and lost overnight, oligarchs are rising, the mafia is thriving, and the police are riddled with corruption. Under heavy snow, unimaginable wealth moves through private vaults, far from any official bank. The streets are filled with stolen cars, blood-stained cash, and armed men in leather coats.

The Perfect Donor

Horror

Marc Delacroix, a 34-year-old lawyer, discovers he possesses an extremely rare genetic profile that makes him compatible with 60% of organ transplant patients - a medical anomaly occurring once in millions. What begins as heroic organ donations to save his sister escalates into a nightmarish conspiracy when Marc realizes he's been marked for death by an international organ trafficking network that orchestrates "accidents" to harvest all his organs at once. Plot Summary After successfully donating a kidney to his sister Emma, Marc becomes a media darling and medical marvel. Multiple hospitals contact him for donations - bone marrow, liver segments, plasma. But mysterious accidents begin plaguing his life: brake failures, poisoned food, electrical hazards, each occurring right after he refuses or delays a procedure.

Obsidian code

Thriller

In 2034, a secret organization known as The Black Division infiltrates the world’s largest artificial intelligence networks to trigger a global energy crisis. Their goal: create a controlled chaos that will allow them to rewrite the world order in their favor. Agent Alexei Moreau, a master of psychological infiltration and a former high-profile thief, is recruited by an off-the-record black-ops cell that exists on no official database. His mission: dismantle The Black Division within 96 hours, before the world’s power grids collapse beyond recovery. The twist—his enemy is not a single individual, but a constantly evolving system powered by AI itself. To succeed, Alexei must assume multiple identities, infiltrate a high-stakes gala in Dubai, thwart a deep-sea cyber-heist beneath the North Sea, and confront a past that The Black Division seems to know far too well.
